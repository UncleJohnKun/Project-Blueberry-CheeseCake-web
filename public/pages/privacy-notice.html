Privacy Notice

Last updated: September 27, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Create an account or use our educational platform
  • Submit assignments, quizzes, or other educational content
  • Communicate with us via contact forms or email
  • Participate in surveys or feedback sessions

This may include your name, email address, educational institution, grade level, and learning progress data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our educational services
  • Track learning progress and provide personalized educational content
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To authorized school administrators and teachers (for student accounts)
  • To comply with legal requirements or protect our rights
  • To service providers who assist in operating our platform

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Student Privacy (COPPA & FERPA Compliance)

We are committed to protecting student privacy and comply with applicable laws including:

  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA)
  • We do not collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Educational records are protected and only shared with authorized personnel

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of certain communications
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent (where applicable)

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and provide personalized content. Our platform uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly, including authentication and security features.
  • Functional Cookies: Enable enhanced functionality such as language preferences and dashboard layout customization.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website to improve our services.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to track visitors and display relevant advertisements (if applicable).

You have full control over your cookie preferences. You can:

  • Manage your cookie preferences through our Cookie Preferences Center
  • Control cookies through your browser settings
  • Opt out of analytics and marketing cookies while keeping essential functionality

Note: Disabling certain cookies may impact the functionality of our platform.

8. Changes to This Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Notice on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
